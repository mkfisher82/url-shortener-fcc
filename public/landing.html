Url Shortener Microservice

To get a shortened url:

Add the suffix '/new/yoururl' to the end of this url.

For example: https://fierce-fjord-32039.herokuapp.com/new/https://www.freecodecamp.org

To use a shortened url:

Simply enter the shortened url in your favorite browser and you will be redirected to your website.

For example: https://fierce-fjord-32039.herokuapp.com/6957
